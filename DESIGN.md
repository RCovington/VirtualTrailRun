## ðŸŒ² Design Philosophy

### Nature-Inspired
The design evokes the feeling of being on a natural trail:
- **Forest greens** represent trees and foliage
- **Tans** represent dirt trails and natural pathways
- **Orange** represents the energy of sunrise/sunset during outdoor runs

### Athletic Energy
- Bold, vibrant orange for action elements
- Clean, modern typography
- Dynamic motion indicators
- High contrast for readability during workouts

### Accessibility
- Strong color contrast ratios (WCAG AA compliant)
- Large touch targets for mobile use
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Intuitive iconography

## ðŸŽ¨ Component Styling

### Buttons
- **Primary Actions:** Forest green (#2D5016) with white text
- **Active/Selected:** Vibrant orange (#FF6B35)
- **Secondary Actions:** Muted gray with lower prominence
- **Hover States:** Darker green or lighter orange

### Cards & Panels
- **Background:** White or light tan (#E8D4B8)
- **Borders:** Tan (#D4A574)
- **Accent Borders:** Orange left-border for emphasis

### Data Display
- **Stats Values:** Orange (#FF6B35) - high visibility
- **Labels:** Muted green-gray (#6B7456)
- **Progress Bars:** Green-to-orange gradient

### Video Overlay
- **Distance Display:** Orange text on dark semi-transparent background
- **Positioning:** Top-right, non-intrusive
- **Glass effect:** Backdrop blur for modern look
